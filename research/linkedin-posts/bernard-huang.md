## Post 1 — AI Search Engines Are Not All the Same

* **Author:** Bernard Huang
* **Platform:** LinkedIn
* **Publication date:** March 4, 2026
* **Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bernardjhuang_everyone-keeps-saying-ai-is-the-new-search-activity-7435061789035253761-lv81
* **Collection method:** Manually collected from the author’s public LinkedIn post.
* **Annotation:** Bernard Huang explains that AI search platforms use different retrieval systems, data sources, ranking mechanisms, and models. As a result, brands cannot rely on one universal AI-search optimization strategy.

### Key Takeaways

* Different AI platforms can produce different answers for the same query.
* Visibility in one AI search engine does not guarantee visibility in another.
* Content teams should evaluate performance across multiple platforms.
* AI-search optimization requires understanding how each system retrieves and presents information.
* A diversified content and brand-presence strategy is more reliable than optimizing for a single model.
