* **Author:** Kevin Indig
* **Platform:** LinkedIn
* **Publication date:** February 26, 2026

* **Annotation:** Kevin Indig explains that adopting AI SEO is not only a technical challenge. It also requires education, organizational change, realistic expectations, and a gradual implementation process.

### Key Takeaways

* AI SEO adoption should be treated as an organizational transition rather than a simple tool change.
* Teams should prioritize progress over perfection by moving through pilot, expansion, and embedded adoption phases.
* Leaders should be honest about what is still uncertain while acting on the risks and opportunities that are already visible.
* AI visibility should be translated into business outcomes such as brand recall, influence, and pipeline.
* Strong change management can significantly increase the chance that AI-related initiatives succeed.
