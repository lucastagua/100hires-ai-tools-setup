### Key Takeaways

* A loss of Google visibility can be followed by a loss of citations in AI search systems.
* Short-term tactics designed to manipulate AI results can create long-term risks for established brands.
* Sustainable AI-search visibility still depends on strong SEO fundamentals, trust, authority, and useful content.
* Content teams should avoid tactics that generate rapid growth but leave the site vulnerable to algorithm updates or spam enforcement.
* The strongest strategy is one that continues producing citations after search engines and AI platforms improve their spam detection.
