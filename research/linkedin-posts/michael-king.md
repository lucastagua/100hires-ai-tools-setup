### Key Takeaways

* AI search optimization extends beyond ranking individual webpages.
* Brands need visibility across third-party publications, communities, structured data sources, and the wider open web.
* Traditional on-page SEO remains relevant but is only one part of AI-search visibility.
* Content teams should optimize information for retrieval, interpretation, citation, and answer generation.
