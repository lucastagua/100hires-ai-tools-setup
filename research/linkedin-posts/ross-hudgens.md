## Post 1 — How Content Teams Should Approach AI Adoption

* **Author:** Ross Hudgens
* **Platform:** LinkedIn
* **Publication date:** May 28, 2026
* **Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosshudgens_ceocmo-do-we-use-profound-are-we-using-activity-7465776563909890049-fHjh
* **Collection method:** Manually collected from the author’s public LinkedIn post.
* **Annotation:** Ross Hudgens explains that content teams should actively evaluate AI tools and develop a clear position on where they add value. He recommends using AI for research, editing, and ideation while keeping human writers and reviewers responsible for the final output.

### Key Takeaways

* Marketing teams should evaluate AI tools instead of dismissing them without investigation.
* AI can support research, editing, and ideation without replacing human judgment.
* Human writers and reviewers should remain responsible for the final published content.
* Teams need a clear and well-reasoned explanation of where and why they use AI.
* A structured source of truth containing brand voice, positioning, and proof points can help produce more differentiated AI-assisted content.
