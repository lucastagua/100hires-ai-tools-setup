## Post 1 — B2B AEO Beyond “Just SEO”

* **Author:** Steve Toth
* **Platform:** LinkedIn
* **Publication date:** June 5, 2026
* **Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/stevetothjr_getting-cited-in-ai-overviews-is-just-seo-activity-7468647893676232704-VpE_
* **Collection method:** Manually collected from the author’s public LinkedIn post.
* **Annotation:** Steve Toth argues that traditional SEO techniques help content earn AI citations, but B2B answer-engine optimization must also ensure that AI systems accurately represent a product’s capabilities, pricing, integrations, compliance, and suitability during the buyer journey.

### Key Takeaways

* Clear, declarative writing and directly retrievable answers help content earn AI citations.
* Earning citations is only the foundation of B2B AI-search visibility.
* Buyers use AI systems to evaluate product fit, pricing, integrations, security, and other deal-breaking requirements.
* Incorrect or incomplete AI representations can disqualify a company before a buyer reaches its website.
* Brands must influence how they are described across multiple AI platforms, not only Google.
