* **Annotation:** Tim Soulo describes how marketers can turn one-off AI conversations into reusable applications for backlink analysis, content research, marketing copy, task management, and AI-visibility reporting.

### Key Takeaways

* Repetitive marketing workflows can be converted into small, reusable AI applications.
* Custom tools can perform focused tasks faster and at a very low operating cost.
* The main barrier to adoption is often identifying which processes can be automated, rather than technical complexity.
* AI tools should be adapted to the user’s specific workflow instead of copied without context.
* Marketing expertise remains essential for designing useful tools and validating their output.
